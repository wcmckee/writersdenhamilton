The event that just took place was onboard a ship traveling through unknown space with its engines switched off. It involved two main characters of the story. Everything is one big cycle. Whatever happens, happens. If you attempt to change it, it will fail. Detective is sent back to where he wakes to a world inhabited by humans. The humans find the alien dna in his brain leading to extraction and development of ai systems.

It's a ever lasting circle. When will it stop?
